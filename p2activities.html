HOW TO HELP

Angat Buhay has initiated feeding and nutrition programs, housing projects, classroom construction, scholarships, disaster relief and rehabilitation, livelihood opportunities. Among the beneficiaries are the survivors of the 2017 Marawi siege, who were given hygiene kits, medicines, psychological aid, food packs and materials to rebuild their homes. The OVP also built temporary shelters.
